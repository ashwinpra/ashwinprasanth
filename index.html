About me

I am Ashwin Prasanth, a Grade 12 student at The Indian High School Dubai.

Having studied in 3 different schools in India and in the United Arab Emirates, I have amassed a plethora of invaluable experiences and memories, which I believe have helped me become the person I am now.

Over the years, I have shifted my interest from Electronics and Circuits to Robotics to finally Math and Computer Science, which I aspire to major in.
